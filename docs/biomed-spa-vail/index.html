05 Priority Recommendations
01 Fix the "My Site 1" Page Titles — Today
Every interior and product page needs a real, keyword-rich title: "MedSpa Services — Botox, Morpheus8 & Body Contouring | BioMed Spa Vail," "Spa Services in Vail Village | BioMed Spa Vail," and so on. This is a free, same-day edit in Wix that removes an obvious unfinished-site signal and immediately improves how every page ranks and appears in search.
02 Claim and Fully Build the Google Business Profile
Verify and complete the GBP — category, hours, service list, the Boulevard booking link, and a strong set of photos (treatment rooms, results, the Manor Vail setting). For a med spa this is the single highest-leverage findability move: it drives "med spa near me" and Maps results and is a primary source AI assistants cite for local recommendations.
03 Launch a Review Engine
Med-spa decisions hinge on reviews. Use Boulevard to automatically send a post-visit text or email asking happy patients to leave a Google review. Going from a handful of reviews to 50+ over one season transforms the trust signal — and review volume directly feeds both Google ranking and AI citations.
04 Build an FAQ + Treatment Content Layer (GEO)
Publish clear pages and FAQs answering the questions patients actually search: "How much does Morpheus8 cost in Vail?" "What's the downtime for BodyTite?" "Am I a candidate for HRT?" "How does medical weight loss work?" This is exactly the written, indexable content that earns organic search traffic and gets quoted in AI-generated answers — the gap between BioMed and the competitors who outrank it.
05 Add a Bilingual (English / Spanish) Layer
No med spa in the valley serves Spanish-speaking clients well, and Eagle County's market is substantially bilingual. A Spanish version of the key service and booking pages opens an entire underserved audience and differentiates BioMed in a way no competitor currently matches. This is Doble AI's signature capability.
06 Add an AI Booking & Treatment Concierge
An AI chat (and optional voice) concierge would answer treatment, pricing, candidacy, and downtime questions and guide patients into the Boulevard booking flow 24/7 — including the after-hours and weekend window when the spa is closed (it shuts at 5pm) but buyers are researching. Bilingual from day one, it captures leads that currently bounce.
07 Turn Instagram Into a Growth Channel
Aesthetics is made for Instagram — before/afters, treatment explainers, provider features, and the Vail Village setting. A consistent three-post-per-week cadence with a defined aesthetic and local tagging would compound 159 followers into a real audience. The content already exists in the treatment room; it just needs to be captured and posted with intent.
08 Move to a Branded Email Address
Replace the free Gmail with a branded address (e.g. info@biomedspavail.com) and confirm contact-form and booking notifications route reliably to it. A Gmail contact undercuts the premium positioning, and a misconfigured form means leads can silently disappear — a quiet revenue leak worth closing.
