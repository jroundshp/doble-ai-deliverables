05 Top 5 Priority Recommendations
01 Claim & Build a Google Business Profile
This is the highest-leverage move available. A complete Google Business Profile with photos, services listed, and accurate hours will put this business on the map — literally — for local search queries like "excavation Eagle County" and "septic installation western Colorado." Free to set up, with immediate and durable impact on local visibility.
02 Fix the Website — Remove the Typo, Add a Contact Form
The live typo on the homepage is the first thing a new prospect sees. Fix it immediately. Then add a simple contact or quote request form so inbound leads don't have to call cold — reducing friction dramatically increases conversion from organic and directory traffic.
03 Claim Yelp, Angi, and HomeAdvisor Listings
These are the platforms homeowners and general contractors actively use to find and vet excavation bids. A verified listing with the A+ BBB reference and Facebook review record as seed content immediately places this business in the consideration set it is currently missing from entirely.
04 Move Facebook Reviewers to Google
Ask current satisfied customers — starting with those 12 Facebook reviewers — to leave a Google review once the Business Profile is live. Five Google reviews will do more for local search visibility than 50 Facebook reviews. The social proof already exists; it just needs to be in the right place.
05 Build a Simple Project Photo Gallery
Add 10–15 before-and-after photos to the website. Earthmoving, septic, site clearing — anything visual. Prospects evaluating contractors respond strongly to project evidence. This is inexpensive to execute and dramatically increases trust for anyone landing on the site from any source.
