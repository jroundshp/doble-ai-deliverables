05 The Plan
01Lead with the bilingual + broadcaster + brand triad
The homepage and About open on the one combination nobody else has: Spanish-first delivery, BBC/ABC documentary credibility, and outdoor-brand work. This becomes the headline, not a footnote buried in the bio.
02Re-base everything to Denver–Boulder
Every page reads as Colorado-based, with Peru and the Andes kept as expedition credibility — not as home. This is the single biggest fix for getting found in your actual market.
03Build Spanish-language location and intent pages
English search terms are saturated; their Spanish equivalents face almost no competition. Pages like "fotógrafo de bodas de aventura en Colorado" can own search that no competitor is even contesting.
04Turn your 11 films into named case studies
Client, brief, outcome — the way Roo Smith presents work. It's the biggest credibility upgrade available, and it's what brand buyers actually read before they hire. A short bilingual blog reinforces it.
05Add packages, anchor pricing, and a real inquiry path
Almost the entire field hides behind "email me." Even a "from $X" range plus clear packages beats them on trust. For the wedding lane, get listed on The Knot and WeddingWire and start collecting reviews — the signal couples filter on first.
06Add FAQ + structured content so AI search cites you
Question-phrased sections with FAQ schema give ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews something to quote — in both languages. An open channel, and one where being early is the whole advantage.
